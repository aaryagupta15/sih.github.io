WHAT DO WE AIM

We're developing an app to assist specially-abled people in improving their personal and professional lives, with the goal of making their lives more meaningful and hopeful in the long run. Our software will help individuals schedule their daily routine and activities depending on their special needs, as well as collaborate with NGOs to help them with fundraising initiatives. Also, a component of our app will focus on increasing the employability of persons with disabilities by identifying their areas of specialisation and interest, training them according to industry standards, and assisting them in finding work. They are only required to pay us our training cost once they have been hired. We will work with companies that share our mission and urge them to hire specially-abled personnel who have been trained by us. Our teaching methods and tactics may vary depending on their unique needs, and we will modify them over time as additional critiques and suggestions come in. Individuals with special needs and their guardians would receive free/ low-cost counselling and treatments. The fact that we will just charge our cost of service with no additional profit upfront will make our treatments and counselling superior to existing offline options. They will only have to pay our profits once they see positive results from our treatment and counselling. This will foster consumer trust in our endeavour, which will aid in the long-term development of a brand.

WE UNDERSTAND YOU

Without a doubt, our impact on society will be significant and will change the lives of many.
